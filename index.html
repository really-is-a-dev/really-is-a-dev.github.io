If you wish to get your own sub-domain then contact @ukriu on discord or any other messenging platform with your requested sub-domain and the records needed.
You can find ukriu's other socials at ukriu.com.

This is only possible by and with the people at is-a.dev who provide free sub-domains to all developers! Check out their github to register your own is-a.dev subdomain here!


Usage on GitHub:

Go on any repository for GitHub with Pages support and add your sub-domain.

For Example:
If I wanted the domain ukriu.really.is-a.dev for my GitHub Pages website then I would need to navigate to The Repository > Settings > Pages > Custom Domain and enter the sub-domain in the field and hit Save.

Non-Github use:

For any website not hosted on GitHub, you can just message me and I'll add the records or redirections of the domain to your website! So If you have a Cloudflare Pages website, you can tell me to add CNAME records and I'll get back to you as soon as possible!
